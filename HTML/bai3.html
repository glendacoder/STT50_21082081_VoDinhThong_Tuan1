One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re -evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, adn readlizes the harsh existence of the homeless. and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Film. The Pelican Brief. and The Client.
